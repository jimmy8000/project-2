My Hobbies

When I’m not working, I’m usually building something, learning something, or taking photos. I like hobbies that mix creativity with a little structure.

💻 Building Projects

I enjoy making web apps and polishing UI until it feels smooth. I’m a fan of Next.js + Tailwind, and I like when a layout feels “clean” without being boring.

  • Favorite part: designing the structure of pages and components
  • Also fun: fixing bugs that used to annoy me
  • Always learning: better patterns and cleaner code

📸 Photography

I like taking portraits and clean shots with good lighting. Sometimes it’s people, sometimes it’s my pets, and sometimes it’s just Chicago being Chicago.

  • Style: simple, crisp, and well-framed
  • Favorite subject: anything with personality
  • Bonus: editing photos is weirdly relaxing

🧠 Memory + Languages

I’m into memory techniques (Nelson Dellis’s approach) and I’m learning Spanish. I like training my brain the same way I train a skill in tech: small, consistent sessions.

  • Memory: palaces, systems, and daily practice
  • Spanish: vocabulary + short speaking practice
  • Goal: steady progress that actually sticks

My “Good Week” Routine

  • 2–3 short project sessions (ship something small).
  • One photo walk (even 20 minutes counts).
  • Spanish practice most days (tiny is fine).
  • One memory session to keep the habit alive.
